# Privacy Policy

## 1. General
This app functions solely as a remote control for compatible televisions over the local network.

The developer of this app does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

## 2. Network Communication
The app communicates only with compatible televisions within the local network (LAN/Wi-Fi).

Only technical information required to control the television is transmitted (for example remote control commands).

This communication occurs directly between the app and the television.
No data is transmitted to external servers.

## 3. Data Storage
The app does not store any personal data.

Depending on usage, certain technical settings may be stored locally on the device, such as:

- the IP address of a connected television
- pairing information required to connect to the television

These data remain exclusively on the user's device.

## 4. Permissions
The app may require network access in order to discover and control televisions within the local network.

No sensitive permissions are used.

## 5. Third-Party Services
This app does not use third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This privacy policy may be updated if necessary to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements.
