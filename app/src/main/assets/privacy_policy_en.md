## 1. General
This app is a remote control for Philips TVs in your local network.

The developer of this app does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

## 2. Network Communication
The app communicates exclusively with the Philips TV configured by the user.

Only the technical commands necessary for TV control are transmitted (e.g. channel changes, volume, navigation).

Communication takes place directly between your device and the TV. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## 3. Data Storage
The app does not store any personal data.

The following technical settings are stored locally on your device:

- IP address of the Philips TV
- Pairing certificate for authentication

This data remains exclusively on your device.

## 4. Permissions
The app requires network access to communicate with the Philips TV on your local network.

No other sensitive permissions are used.

## 5. Third Parties
This app does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising.
